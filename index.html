Who We Are

Stryde Run Club, Shillong is an active running community that organises weekend runs and fitness events in various locations across Shillong. We usually meet early on weekend mornings to catch the sunrise and the sweat.

Exact warm-up and flag-off times (around 6:00 AM and 6:30 AM) and registration links are announced on our Instagram. Stryde is known for its welcoming, inclusive atmosphere where beginners and experienced runners can connect, move, and grow together.
